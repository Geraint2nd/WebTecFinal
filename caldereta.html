Kaldereta

What is Kaldereta?
Ninong Ry's Beef Kaldereta is a flavorful Filipino stew featuring tender beef simmered in a rich, savory tomato-based sauce. The dish is made distinct by the inclusion of liver spread, which adds a creamy and robust depth to the sauce, making it a staple for special occasions and family gatherings. This hearty dish typically includes well-marbled beef cuts like brisket or round, which slow-cook into a tender, juicy texture. Vegetables such as potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers complement the savory flavors, while the optional addition of grated cheese or coconut milk can further enhance the dish's richness.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

25-30 mins.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1 : Prepare Beef

Sear the beef chunks in a hot pan with oil until browned. Remove and set aside.

step 2: Sauté Aromatics:

In the same pan, sauté onions, garlic, and bay leaves until fragrant.

step 3: Cook the Sauce Base:

Add tomato paste and cook for a minute to enhance its flavor and pour in the tomato sauce and mix well.

step 3: Incorporate Liver Spread:

Stir in the liver spread or pâté. This adds richness and depth to the sauce.

step 6: Add Vegetables:

Toss in the potatoes and carrots. Cook until tender. Add the bell peppers toward the end to maintain their vibrant color and slight crunch.

step 7: Optional Additions:

Mix in grated cheese for a creamy, indulgent twist.

step 8: Adjust Seasoning:

Taste the dish and adjust salt and pepper as needed.

ingredients

1 kg beef (preferably brisket or round), cut into chunks

1 cup tomato sauce

2 tbsp tomato paste

1 can liver spread or fresh liver pâté

1-2 carrots, sliced into chunks

2-3 potatoes, cubed

1 red bell pepper, sliced

1 green bell pepper, sliced

1 cup beef broth or water

1 medium onion, chopped

4-5 cloves garlic, minced

3-4 tbsp soy sauce

2-3 bay leaves

Salt and pepper, to taste

1/4 cup grated cheese (optional, for added creaminess)

Cooking oil
