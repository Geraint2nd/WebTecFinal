Bulalo

How to Make Sizzling Bulalo
Bulalo is a succulent beef marrow soup cooked simply by boiling all the ingredients on a low simmering heat. This ultimate Filipino comfort food contains a good chunk of beef shank boiled in water with onions, garlic, peppercorns, and salt. Vegetables such as cabbage, corn, and potatoes can be included.

prep time

1 - 2 hrs.

cook time

20 mins.

serving

8 servings

instructions:

step 1 : Prepare and Boil the Beef:

In a large pot, add water, beef shanks, salt, onion, and black pepper. Bring to a boil while skimming off scum from the surface. Lower the heat, cover, and simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the beef is tender. Reserve the broth for the gravy.

step 2:Make the Gravy:

In a pan, melt 4 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the flour and whisk until smooth and golden brown. Slowly pour in 2 cups of reserved beef broth, whisking continuously to prevent lumps. Add soy sauce and peppercorns, then cook until thickened. Adjust seasoning with salt if needed.

step 3: Assemble on the Sizzling Plate:

Heat a sizzling plate (or cast iron skillet) on the stovetop or in the oven. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter on the hot plate. Arrange the cooked beef shanks on the plate and pour the gravy generously over the meat. Garnish with fried garlic.

ingredients:

For the Beef and Broth:

800 to 900g beef shank (bulalo)

Water (enough to boil the beef)

1 tablespoon salt (or seasoning granules)

5 to 6 tablespoons milk, as needed

1 onion, chopped

1 teaspoon whole black pepper

For the Gravy:

4 tablespoons butter

4 tablespoons all-purpose flour

2 cups beef broth (reserved from boiling the beef)

2 tablespoons soy sauce

1 teaspoon whole peppercorns

For Serving:

2 tablespoons butter or margarine

Fried garlic (for garnish)
